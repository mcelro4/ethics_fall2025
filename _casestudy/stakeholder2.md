 **Developers**

    A Developer plays a key role in sports betting algorithms as they directly shape how the system works, ultimately influencing user behavior, profit models, and ethical outcomes. Developers recommend algortihms that will seem appealing to live betters and gamblers in general. With a lot of control, developers have the power to impliment safeguards for users when noticable splurge spending is happenng, alongside other warning signs of harmful behavior.
