    The Gambler


    There are a lot of roles that gamblers play within the sports betting community. They spend their money gambling which keeps apps running. Gamblers are the data for analyst to collect in order to implement the most effective algorithms. Time spent on apps, money lost vs earned, time in between bets etc. Gamblers also shape the algorithms that they see. Spending behaviors, money spent, and overall experience are tailored towards algorithmic design.
