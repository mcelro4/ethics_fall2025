Algoithmic Persuasion in Sports Betting: How Data-Driven systems influence User Gambling Behavior. 

## Abstract

This case study explores the algorthmic mechanisms deployed by sports betting platforms to attract and retain users. It examines how data driven personalization, predictive modeling, and behavioral reinforcement tenchniques intertwine into interface design to encourage betting engagement. Honing in on research methds, behavioral patterns, and digital ethics, this study analyzes how algorithmic systems influence users decisions to place bets and contribute to patterns of compulsive betting. The findings gathered over the course of research aim to shed light on to the online gambling inustry and the ethical frameworks/ concerns with the industry. Hopefully propising new ways that corperations can include transparency and accountability in sports betting technologies.
